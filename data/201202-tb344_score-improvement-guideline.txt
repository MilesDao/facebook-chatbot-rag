NOTICE
On Issuing the Guidelines on Score Improvement Examinations

Socialist Republic of Vietnam
Independence – Freedom – Happiness

Hanoi, December 2, 2020

Legal Basis

Pursuant to the Academic Policy for Undergraduate Degree issued together with Decision No. 627/QĐ-DHKHCN dated September 10, 2019 by the Rector of the University of Science and Technology of Hanoi;
Based on students’ expectations for improving study results;

The University of Science and Technology of Hanoi (USTH) issues the guidelines on score improvement examinations as follows:

PART I: Conditions of Score Improvement

Students who have a final examination score of 10.0 or higher are allowed to register for a score improvement examination in order to increase the cumulative average grade.

The maximum number of credits registered for score improvement examinations is 10 credits per semester.

In special cases, students may submit an application to USTH for consideration.

There is no limit to the number of times a student may take score improvement examinations for each module.

PART II: Principles of Score Improvement

Students who do not attend the examination without an appropriate reason shall receive a grade of 0 (zero).

The score of a score improvement examination is considered the module score of that improvement examination.

Process (continuous assessment) scores are preserved for reference purposes only and are not used in calculating the module score of score improvement examinations.

After participating in score improvement examinations, the highest module score among all examinations taken by the student shall be used.

For score improvement modules, students only pay examination fees and do not pay tuition fees.

Students are only allowed to cancel the registration for score improvement examinations before paying the fees.

Improved scores are not used for USTH scholarship assessment.

PART III: Procedure of Score Improvement Examination
Step 1

Students check the final examination schedule, complete the registration form for score improvement examinations, and submit it to the Department of Academic Affairs (DAA) at least two (2) weeks before the final examination.

Step 2

Students pay the examination fees at the Department of Accounting – Finance before the score improvement examination.

Step 3

Within three (3) days after registration for the score improvement examination, students must bring the Payment Receipt and Registration Form to the Department of Academic Affairs (DAA) to record the payment of the fee.

If students do not complete this step, they are considered to have voluntarily cancelled their registration for score improvement examinations.

PART IV: Organization of Score Improvement Examinations

Students register for score improvement examinations in accordance with the procedure stated above.

Score improvement examinations are organized at the same time as the final and retake examinations of USTH, except for special cases decided by the Rector Board.

In cases where score improvement examinations are not organized or students are not qualified to register for score improvement examinations, students shall be informed accordingly.

Effectiveness

This regulation takes effect from the date of issuance.
